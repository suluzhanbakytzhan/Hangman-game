Hangman Game

How to Play

Welcome to the Hangman Game! Here's how to play:

  • Select a theme from the options above to begin the game.
  • A hidden word will appear, represented by underscores (_ _ _ _).
  • Guess the word by clicking on letters. Correct guesses will reveal the letters.
  • Incorrect guesses will add parts to the hangman drawing.
  • You lose if the full hangman is drawn before the word is guessed (6 wrong guesses).
  • You win if you correctly guess all the letters in the word!
  • Use the "Reveal Letter" button for hints (up to 3 hints per game).

Good luck and have fun!
